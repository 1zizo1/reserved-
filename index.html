about us

Reserved, is a convenient and easy-to-use platform that allows users to book reservations for a variety of services in Egypt. From civil sector services such as the Civil Status Sector, Traffic Sector, and banks, to entertainment services such as cinema reservations, our platform provides a one-stop-shop for all your reservation needs. Our goal is to simplify the reservation process, saving you time and effort, and ensuring that you can quickly and easily secure your spot for the services you need. With Reserved It, you can have peace of mind knowing that your reservation is confirmed and that you will receive the services you require.
