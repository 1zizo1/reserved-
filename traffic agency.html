traffic sector

The Traffic Sector in Egypt is a government agency responsible for managing and regulating traffic on the country's roads and highways. Its main responsibilities include enforcing traffic laws and regulations, investigating and responding to traffic accidents, and promoting road safety through public awareness campaigns and road safety measures. The Traffic Sector plays a critical role in ensuring the safety of drivers, passengers, and pedestrians on the roads, and its efforts are essential for reducing traffic accidents and fatalities in the country.
